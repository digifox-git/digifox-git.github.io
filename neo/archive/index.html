About the site...

  I started "digifox's cool site" on Neocities back in 2023 as a way for me to get into programming and design. It was a little rough, though, since I didn't have much to put on it. The site has gradually become bigger and bigger as I discover more about my passions and identity. I may call the current layout "version 3," but I've honestly redesigned the site at least a thousand times. I'm not very good at decision-making, you see... Anyways, things settled down a bit though when I bought the digifox.space domain in 2024 and moved to Github Pages. Basically this whole website is one big project, and every iteration is a milestone that shows how I've improved.
