Abiotic Factor

"Abiotic Factor is a survival crafting experience for 1-6 players set in the depths of an underground research facility. My Review...

Played this with some friends and we had a great time! I was a little frustrated at first since I wasn't really sure what to do, but after I got a hang on everything it was a lot of fun. It's a bit like Half-Life in terms of looks and plot, and I think it really helped to sell this game for me!
