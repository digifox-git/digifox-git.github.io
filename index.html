About the Site

A large part of digifox.space was created using Adobe GoLive, a piece of software designed to make creating websites easy with no coding knowledge required. Originally called GoLive Cyberstudio, it was aquired by Adobe in 1999 and rereleased on Macintosh and Windows computers under the name Adobe GoLive. GoLive replaced PageMill as Adobe's proprietary website-building tool for about 6 years until it was itself was replaced by Dreamweaver due to Adobe's aquisition of Macromedia. If you'd like to try out GoLive or any other older software, check out the Web Links page!
